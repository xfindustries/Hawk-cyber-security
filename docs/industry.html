Government & Public Sector

Agencies and public organizations face strict mandates and mission-critical uptime requirements. We align programs to frameworks like NIST 800-53/171 and FedRAMP to protect sensitive data, prevent service disruption, and maintain continuity.

  • NIST, RMF, and ATO support; control inheritance & continuous monitoring
  • Identity-centric defense: zero trust, privileged access, logging & detections
  • Incident response playbooks and tabletop exercises

Financial Services

High-value targets require layered, measurable controls. We protect transactions, reduce fraud, and meet PCI DSS and privacy obligations.

  • PCI DSS gap analysis, remediation roadmaps, and attestation support
  • Threat detection engineering (SIEM/XDR) and fraud monitoring integrations
  • Data protection and key management, encryption at-rest/in-transit

Manufacturing & OT

Interconnected production lines and IoT increase exposure. We protect operational tech, minimize downtime, and safeguard IP.

  • OT segmentation, asset discovery, and anomaly detection
  • Vulnerability management tuned for patch windows & safety constraints
  • Supplier risk and secure firmware/PLC change control

Energy & Infrastructure

Critical services demand resilience. We harden controls, prevent service disruption, and align with sector standards (e.g., ISO, NERC-CIP).

  • Identity, access, and segmentation for remote operations
  • Backup, recovery, business continuity, and crisis communication testing
